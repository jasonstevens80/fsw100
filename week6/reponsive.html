Story

Bridge City Tacos came about when a love for Austin style breakfast tacos became too much for one person to keep to himself.

Jason grew up eating breakfast tacos multiple times a week for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

He moved to Pittsburgh over 10 years ago and has been making them in his home since.

He has decided to share his love for breakfast tacos with the Pittsburgh community and brings you "Bridge City Tacos."
