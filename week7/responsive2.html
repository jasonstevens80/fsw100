Story

Bridge City Tacos opened in 2019 but our story began long before in Austin, Texas. That’s where our founder Jason grew up and first fell in love with the magical flavors of the breakfast taco. Years later, his childhood memories mixed with Pittsburgh’s friendly vibes to inspire Bridge City Taco's food and the way we share it. The result is an ambitious menu, prepared by hand every day. A healthy obsession with both the quality of our ingredients and the quality of our lives. We extend a warm welcome to anyone who’s hungry for something different.
