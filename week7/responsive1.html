Story

Bridge City Tacos came about when a love for Austin style breakfast tacos became too much for one person to keep to himself. Jason grew up eating breakfast tacos multiple times a week for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Perfecting his craft in various kitchens in and around Austin, Jason learned from some of the best chefs in town. He moved to Pittsburgh over 10 years ago and started making them in his home for friends. He has decided to share his love for breakfast tacos with the Pittsburgh community and brings you "Bridge City Tacos."
